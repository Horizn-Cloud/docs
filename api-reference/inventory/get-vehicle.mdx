---
title: "Get vehicle"
openapi: "GET /inventory/{vin}"
description: "Get full details for a specific vehicle"
---

Returns complete vehicle details including specs, pricing, and photo URLs.

**Scope required:** `inventory:read`

## Path parameters

<ParamField path="id" type="string" required>UUID of the vehicle.</ParamField>
